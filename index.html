I am assistant professor at University of Georgia! I was previously at Harvard as an NSF postdoc, and did my PhD with Robert Friedman at Columbia. I am currently interested in two areas: Degenerations of K3 surfaces and anticanonical pairs, partly motivated by mirror symmetry, and tilings of Riemann surfaces and Hurwitz theory. I love talking about math and meeting new people, so please drop by my office if you feel like chatting. My email address is philip dot engel at uga dot edu. Thanks for visiting!
