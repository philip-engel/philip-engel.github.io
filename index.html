I am an NSF postdoc at Columbia University.  I completed my PhD at Stanford in 2015.

Broadly speaking, I am interested in the interplay between algebraic geometry and number theory (and, to a lesser extent, topology).  Most of my work has focused on using arithmetic techniques to study classical questions about, for example, complex algebraic varieties.
